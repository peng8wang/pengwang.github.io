Research Interests

My research area is mainly in continuous optimization and its application to machine learning, signal processing, and data analysis. I am particularly interested in various analytic issues of optimization problems and algorithms arising from these applications.

Research Experience & Education

I joined the Department of Mathematics at Hong Kong Baptist University on September 1st, 2018 as an assistant professor. From October 2016 to August 2018, I was an Alan Mekler postdoctoral fellow at Simon Fraser University, working with Zhaosong Lu and Tamon Stephen.

I received my Ph.D. in 2015 from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Anthony Man-Cho So. Prior to that, I received my B.S. in Mathematics from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2011.
